  1. Activation - I do these Activation drills/exercises every day before all of my runs and workouts, especially when training hard. They help loosen joins and prepare muscles for high mileage running and any workout. Try Activation Drills
  2. Dynamic Flexibility - Strength & Flexibility is an important part of training for runners no matter the race distance they specialize or race in. I try to do these exercises 2-3 times per week especially when running and training. They help loosen joins and prepare muscles for high mileage running. Try Dynamic Flexibility
  3. Distance Lifting Circuit - I created this workout circuit by combining lifts and exercises I was coached by former coaches and from exercises I added. I believe these specific exercises help strengthen hips, quads, calves, biceps, triceps, and help increase range of motion. These exercises were integral in my Marathon Training and I recommend adding some of them when doing Distance and Marathon Training. Try my Distance Lifting Circuit
  4. Ab Workouts & Circuits - I do lots of different exercises for abs, obliques, and core. I recommend adding any number of these exercises to strengthen abs or core. Start with 4-6 minutes of continuous abs (1 minute per exercise) and work up to 8-10 minutes if possible. Try my 6-10 Minute Ab Circuit
